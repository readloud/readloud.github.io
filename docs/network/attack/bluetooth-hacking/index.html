Bluetooth Hacking

A short-range wireless technology standard that is used for exchanging data between fixed and mobile devices over short distances and building personal area networks.

BlueBorne

A type of attack in which a Bluetooth-enabled device hijacks another Bluetooth device to send spam advertising.
